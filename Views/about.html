ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

  • Stay solid construction is a 100% black youth owned company established this year, out of a solid need to create sustainable employment and participate to the countrys economic mainstream. Stay Solid Construction focuses on empowering the previously disadvantaged individuals by giving them opportunities to acquire experience so they can ultimately reach their full potential. We are a renowned construction company, specializing primarily on building upgrade, commercial buildings and governmental structures, both for private and public sector within the borders of South Africa. Stay solid construction takes pride in its work for best quality service delivery, competitive pricing and just in time delivery. Stay Solid Construction employs Consulting Engineers, Project Managers and Specialist Resource Suppliers to carry out its projects. This strong team experts assists stay solid construction in executing projects of any magnitude while adhering to quality standards and customers specifications.
    Out of all the three sectors of construction which are Building, infrastructure and industrial, Stay Solid Construction will be delving in of them but mainly focusing on building for now because we are still a start-up company.
